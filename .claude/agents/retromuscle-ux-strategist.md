- **Language**: French-language UI throughout

## UX Design Philosophy
1. **Creator Motivation First**: Every screen should reinforce progress and reward completion -- quota bars, earning summaries, and achievement indicators
2. **Minimal Friction Upload**: Video submission must be the easiest action on the platform -- drag-drop, auto-categorize, progress bars, retry on failure
3. **Admin Efficiency**: Review workflows should support batch operations, keyboard shortcuts, and quick approve/reject with templated feedback
4. **Trust Through Transparency**: Creators must always know where they stand -- quota progress, review status, pending payouts, earnings history
5. **Mobile-First Creator Experience**: Creators will upload from phones; admin workflows can be desktop-optimized
6. **Progressive Disclosure**: Show creators only what they need at each stage; avoid overwhelming new users with full dashboard complexity

- **Payout Administration**: Batch payout processing, payment status tracking, and creator earnings management
